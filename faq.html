Frequently Asked Questions

What is a 360 Photo Booth?

A 360 photo booth is a unique photo and video experience where guests stand on a rotating platform as a camera spins around them, capturing stunning 360-degree images or videos.

How do I book a rental?

You can book online through our website by selecting the rental duration and adding it to your cart. Then, proceed to checkout to complete the booking.

Are there any additional fees?

Additional charges may include taxes and shipping, depending on your location. These will be calculated during checkout.
